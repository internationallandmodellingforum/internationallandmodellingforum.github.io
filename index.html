About ILMF

Origins – Land Surface Modelling Summit

ILMF emerged from discussions at the Land Surface Modelling Summit (LSMS), held in Oxford, UK (12–14 September 2022). The summit highlighted the need for an ongoing forum where modelling centres and researchers can work together on shared priorities.

The Forum supports:

  • Coordinated development of land and land–atmosphere models
  • Exchange of ideas, tools, datasets, and best practice
  • Promotion of workshops, meetings, and job opportunities
  • Support for working groups on specific topics

Participating model groups

Modelling groups represented at LSMS included (among others):

  • JULES
  • ORCHIDEE
  • JSBACH
  • LPJ-GUESS
  • TESSEL
  • CABLE
  • CLM
  • ELM
  • GFDL-LM
  • Matsiro
  • GISS-LM
  • CoLM
  • CLASSIC
  • SURFEX-ISBA

ILMF also connects to complementary initiatives such as AIMES, iLEAPS, and GEWEX/GLASS.

Community & mailing list

How to subscribe

The ILMF mailing list is the main channel for announcements, events, and community discussions.

  1. Go to the ILMF list homepage on JiscMail.
  2. Click Subscribe, enter your details, and submit.
  3. Confirm your subscription via the “OK” link emailed to you.

The list is moderated by Eleanor Blyth and Dave Lawrence. Only subscribers can post; archives are available to members.

About the ILMF mailing list

The mailing list helps researchers collaborate and stay informed by sharing:

  • News and announcements
  • Workshops and meetings
  • Job opportunities
  • Working group coordination

Messages are delivered to all subscribers and stored privately.

Already subscribed?

Post to the list via: ILMF@jiscmail.ac.uk

Subscribers may also receive community surveys and working-group updates. We encourage members to participate.
